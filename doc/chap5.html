5 Regular maps

5.1 Bicontactual regular maps

The names for the maps in this section are from S.E. Wilson's paper of the same title.

5.1-1 Epsilonk
‣ Epsilonk( k )( operation )

Returns: maniplex

Given an integer k, gives the map \epsilon_k, which is \{k,2\}_k when k is even, and \{k,2\}_{2k} when k is odd.

5.1-2 Deltak
‣ Deltak( k )( operation )

Returns: maniplex

Given an integer k, gives the map \delta_k, which is \{2k,2\}/2 when k is even, and \{2k,2\}_{k} when k is odd.
